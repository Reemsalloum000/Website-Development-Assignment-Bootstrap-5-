🔄 Return Policy 🔄

At LifeCare Medical Center, we aim to provide high-quality services and products. This Return Policy explains the terms for returning medical products and requesting refunds.

🔄 Eligibility for Returns

Returns are accepted within 14 days of purchase, provided that the product is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt.

🔄 Refund Process

Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after the returned item is inspected. Refunds will be issued via the original payment method.

↩️ Service Refunds

If you are not satisfied with a service, please contact us within 48 hours of the appointment. Refunds for services will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

🚫 Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, the following items are not eligible for return:

  • Opened medications or supplements
  • Personalized medical devices
  • Diagnostic kits
